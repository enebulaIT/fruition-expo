Application

• It is extensively used for knitted fabric & garments

• Used for making bags, bed sheets

Features

• Cotton is an incredibly strong and durable natural fiber, wet or dry.

• Cotton can absorb up to 27 times its own weight in water and water can evaporate.

• It is soft to the touch and lightweight. And since it's a cellulose fiber, it draws heat away from the body. This property makes cotton garments comfortable to wear at any time of the year.

• Cotton makes a very soft yarn. It is machine washable and over time the cotton will soften with more use.

• Cotton is generally inexpensive, but quality varies with price. Depending on the yarn project.

• Cotton thread shows each stitch in great detail.

• You can also dye cotton yarn without any problems.
