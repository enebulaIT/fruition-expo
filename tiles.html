WALL TILES

When it comes to attractive wall tiles, we set the bar high. We've put together the ultimate selection, which includes everything from hand-painted artisan tiles to truly modern and intriguing shapes and materials.

Wall tiles may completely transform the look and feel of a room. Wall tiles are more handy for bathrooms, kitchens, and other interior areas because they are designed to offer the appropriate touch and feel. With our colourful and elegantly painted finest tiles assortment, you can flourish your inner space in any decor.

FLOOR TILES

Our beautiful floor tiles are well-known, and we offer a range that includes everything from exquisite stones and dramatic designs to tiles that seem like wood or concrete. Unlike furniture or décor, you are unlikely to replace your home's flooring in a hurry. It's even more crucial to make an informed and tasteful decision when it comes to your home's flooring design. In most modern homes, the living room flooring begins at the front door and continues straight into the family room, almost like a red carpet into your home.

PARKING TILES

Because it is also used to store oil cans and lawn tools in addition to parking cars, the parking space has the dirtiest flooring in the house. The dust accumulates as the vehicle travels across the floor, making the area unsanitary. Floor tiles are an excellent way to keep your garage looking like an extension of your house. Tiles in a variety of styles and sizes can be purchased on the internet. The parking floor tiles at MyTyles are designed to withstand harsh weather conditions. For your parking area, we provide a large selection of high-quality, long-lasting floor tiles.

SLAB TILES

These man-made emulations of natural stone slab tiles have opened up doors to new design breakthroughs that we've only been able to envision until now, thanks to their striking beauty and astonishing utility. Our slab tiles provide creative building options for facades and external cladding in any project. Slab tiles are ideal for beautiful feature walls, a streamlined look for flooring, and even a porcelain veneer for furniture because of their slim lines and mechanical resilience.

DECORATIVE TILES

Each tile has been carefully selected to accent and protect your kitchen, bathroom, entryway, and other spaces. Transform the look and feel of your house with our wide range of tile colours, finishes, patterns, and forms.
