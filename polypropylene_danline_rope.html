Description

Danline (also called Polysteel) is formed from a mix of polypropylene and polyethylene. thanks to its strength and abrasion resistance, Danline is especially well matched for ropes that must withstand demanding conditions, especially within the fishing industry. It may also be utilized in marine, industrial, rigging and oil fields. This material doesn't rot or absorb water, resists most acids and alkalis, stabilizes in sunlight, and floats on water
