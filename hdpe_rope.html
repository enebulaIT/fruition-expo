Description

FruitionExpo exports a wide range of high-density polyethylene (HDPE) Monofilament Ropes that have been appreciated in the market due to their high breaking strength and durability. breaking strength is the same in dry and wet conditions. That makes it different than other kinds of rope. The rope is used in various applications in Commercial, Marine Fishing, Domestic Purposes, and others.
